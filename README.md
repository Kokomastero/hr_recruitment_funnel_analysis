RecruitIQ: End-to-End Recruitment Funnel & Cost Analytics
A comprehensive Recruitment Analytics project analyzing the full hiring pipeline, time-to-hire efficiency, offer acceptance and cost of recruitment across a simulated dataset of 800 applications.

Business Problem
Most organizations spend millions on recruitment without visibility into where candidates drop off, which channels deliver the best hires, how long hiring takes across departments or what it costs per hire. This project analyzes a simulated recruitment dataset to uncover inefficiencies in the hiring process and provide actionable recommendations for Talent Acquisition teams.

Key Insights
Recruitment Funnel

Only 62 out of 800 applicants were hired — a conversion rate of just 7.8%
340 candidates were Screened Out — the biggest drop off point in the funnel
LinkedIn is the top application source at 197 but Employee Referral takes the longest to hire at 28.4 days
Male candidates slightly outnumber female candidates in hiring — 37 vs 25

Time to Hire

IT and Finance are the slowest departments to hire at 30.8 and 30.5 days respectively
Sales and Customer Service are the fastest at 20.3 and 20.5 days
Ibadan has the slowest location based time to hire at 28.2 days
CV Library NG is the fastest source at 22 days — worth prioritizing

Offer and Cost

24.39% withdrawal rate is significant — nearly 1 in 4 offers are rejected or withdrawn
Failed Technical Assessment is the #1 rejection reason at 225 — screening criteria may be too strict or misaligned
IT is the most expensive department to hire for at 354.84K per hire
Admin is the cheapest at 99.64K per hire
Procurement has the lowest offer acceptance rate at 60% — needs investigation


Recommendations

Fix the Screening Process — 340 candidates screened out and Failed Technical Assessment as the #1 rejection reason suggests the job requirements or assessment criteria need realignment with actual candidate pools
Reduce Time to Hire in IT and Finance — 30+ days to hire is too slow and risks losing top candidates to competitors. Streamline interview stages for these departments
Address the 24.39% Withdrawal Rate — Salary Mismatch and Better Offer are key withdrawal reasons. Review compensation packages and speed up the offer process to reduce dropoff
Leverage CV Library NG and Company Website — these sources have the fastest time to hire. Increasing investment here could improve overall recruitment efficiency
Investigate Procurement's Low Acceptance Rate — at 60% offer acceptance Procurement is losing 4 in 10 candidates at the final stage. Review the offer package and candidate experience for this department
